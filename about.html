About AFLAD

AFLAD creates a platform for facilitators and trainers
with different professional levels to equip and empower
each other in their interested discipline

We are interested in building a network for facilitators and trainer in Africa where they can share ideas, skills, opportunities with each other.
We also provide trainings, mentorship and publications where member can improve their skills.
